OUR STORY

The Space

The Hot 'N' Fast is a classic Pune located. Established in 2023, the restaurant is beloved around the city as a favorite place for an upscale yet approachable night of dinner and drinks.

Brought to you by Executive Chef Jennifer Puccio and award-winning pune designer Ken Fulk, The Hot 'N' Fast focuses on classic comfort foods with flavorful twists, like Chef Puccio’s signature Marlowe Burger, seasonal poulet vert, brussels sprout chips, and quail egg-topped steak tartare.

The Burger

Topped with cheddar, caramelized onions, bacon and horseradish aïoli, the Marlowe burger is one of San Francisco’s best. An immediate hit when it was introduced to the city in 2010, the Marlowe burger still lives on many of the city’s “Best Burger” lists, including the ones by Eater and The Infatuation.
